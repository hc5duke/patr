Updates:

So, I debated for quite a while about how I wanted to do this. It seems odd to me to ask, but a friend recently pointed out that it couldn't hurt to at least ask. So I'm asking.

Consider clicking that "Donate" button (it links to my paypal account) and sending me a little change to keep the coffee flowing? Even 50 cents will help me justify another cup o' joe, and another hour tracking down why Darkr still doesn't work...

If you don't want to drop a donation my way it's totally fine (no sarcasm, I promise), perhaps consider dropping by my blog at blog.patdavid.net and at least clicking an ad (it's like donating without you paying for it!)

Just remember, this won't affect me at least trying to continue working on Patr (updates will still be just as sporadic and likely to break everything in the world!), and I promise I won't push your requests to the bottom of the queue... :)

Finally, everyone has been fantastic about the feedback and requests, and I really, really appreciate it. Your kind words and support seriously make me want to get this stuff fixed even faster!
